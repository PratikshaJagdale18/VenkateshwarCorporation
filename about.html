About Venkateshwara Corporation

Established in 2022, Venkateshwara Corporation is a trusted service provider specializing in Balcony Sheds, Industrial Sheds, Car Parking Awnings, Terrace Sheds, Tensile Structures, Prefabricated Sheds, Fabrication Services, and more.

Over the years, we have built a strong customer base through our service network across India and our commitment to complete customer satisfaction.

Under the guidance of our CEO, Mr. Nilesh Ashok Konde, the company continues to grow with a solid presence in the market, backed by deep industry experience and reliable project delivery.
